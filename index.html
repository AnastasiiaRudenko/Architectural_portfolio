Profile

Highly creative ambitious architect with 5+ years of experience in residential, commercial and religious architectural projects in different countries such as Ukraine, Russia, Kuwait. In past 5 years finished 12 projects at various stages from Concept design to the Construction Documents, 8 of them either finished or under construction. Headed a Contract administration stage. Consistently completed jobs with 10% - 20% less rework than colleagues, deliver finished product on-time and under-budget. Able to work effectively in pressurized situations and can be relied upon to deliver, irrespective of circumstances. Ready to offer fresh new ideas, my skills and experience to significantly contribute to the ongoing success of your company.
